Gentle oil with a light texture. I give your skin a silky feeling, surprisingly non-oily, but with the right amount of glow. After all, the focus should not be on me, but on your radiant complexion.

  • Extra portion of care counteracts tense skin and gives it a rich glow
  • Very light texture without an oily feeling on the skin
  • Power active ingredient complex made from hemp oil, oat and chamomile extract provides intensive moisture
  • Skin type: Especially for sensitive skin
  • Allergen-reduced perfume oil, 100% vegan and natural

Hemp Oil contains important Omega-6 fatty acids, is cell-regenerating and protects the skin without overgreasing it.

Oat Extract has an anti-inflammatory and calming effect and is particularly suitable for sensitive skin.

Chamomile Extract intensively moisturises, is extra gentle and has an antibacterial effect.

How to use: Apply it to your face in the morning and evening after cleansing (approx. 2-3 drops) or mix it into your day or night cream

Ingredients: Helianthus Annuus Seed Oil*, Prunus Armeniaca Kernel Oil, Prunus Amygdalus Dulcis Oil, Coco-Caprylate, Cannabis Sativa Seed Oil*, Shea Butter Ethyl Esters, Brassica Campestris Sterols, Cetearyl Alcohol, Polyglyceryl-6 Caprylate, Polyglyceryl-4 Caprate, Tocopherol, Glycerin, Aqua, Chamomilla Recutita Flower Extract*, Avena Sativa Kernel Extract*, Hypericum Perforatum Extract*, Alcohol, alpha-Bisabolol, Perfume** * Ingredients from Certified organic agriculture, * * Natural essential oils
